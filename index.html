Welcome to Blackjack! The rules of the game are as follows:

1. In this game, picture cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points, and Aces are worth either 11 or 1.

2. The game starts with each player dealt two cards. If any player has a starting hand worth 21 points, i.e. an Ace with a 10, J, Q or K, they win immediately!

3. You go first, and you get to choose if you want to hit (draw a card) or stand (move on to dealer's turn).

4. During the dealer/computer's turn, it will draw until it has 17 or more points.

5. Once all players have stood, the total point values of hands are compared. Any hand above 21 points is bust and loses. The player with the highest remaining hand wins!

6. Betting has been implemented! You start with 100 chips and must bet 10 or more every turn, before cards are dealt.
